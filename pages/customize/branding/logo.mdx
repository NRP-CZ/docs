# Logo <Difficulty level="easy" />

In this section, you'll learn how to update the logo on your repository site. Your logo is a fundamental element of your brand identity, and ensuring it's prominently displayed within the software platform helps reinforce brand recognition and trust among users.

Follow the steps below to seamlessly integrate your brand's logo into your site.

<Steps>
### Provide a logo SVG asset

Copy your logo image asset to `images` folder inside `branding` folder:

```shell copy
cp my-logo.svg myrepo/ui/branding/semantic-ui/less/images/
```

### Configure theme to use your logo


<Tabs items={["invenio.cfg", "variables"]}>
  <Tabs.Tab>
```python copy
THEME_LOGO="images/my-logo.svg"
```
  </Tabs.Tab>
   <Tabs.Tab>
```python copy
INVENIO_THEME_LOGO="images/my-logo.svg"
```
  </Tabs.Tab>
</Tabs>
</Steps>
